DarkTails - Darker than Fairy Tales

Introducing DarkTails, a gripping suspense game inspired by the renowned "Granny" series, crafted with meticulous care in Roblox Studio. In DarkTails, players are thrust into a chilling, haunted environment where every corner hides a new challenge.

As you navigate through the eerie surroundings, your primary objective is to solve intricate puzzles and evade lurking dangers. The tension escalates with every step you take, as you race against the clock to find your way out before time runs out.

Key features of DarkTails include:

  • Engaging Gameplay Mechanics: Experience a variety of puzzles that test your problem-solving skills and keep you on edge.
  • Immersive Environments: Explore meticulously designed haunted locales that create a truly spine-tingling atmosphere.
  • Multiplayer Capabilities: Team up with friends or face the horrors solo in a multiplayer mode that enhances the thrill of the escape.

DarkTails promises to deliver an exhilarating experience, blending suspenseful gameplay with a captivating narrative and dynamic multiplayer interactions. Are you ready to uncover the secrets and survive the night?
